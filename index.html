About

My name is Rachael and I am a person. I live in Seattle and I like coding, even if right in this moment I want to destroy whoever dreamed up CSS.

I was born in NYC, but only lived there for a few years. My family moved around a lot when I was a little kid for job stuff. After New York, we lived in Washington D.C., before moving to the San Francisco Bay Area.

It is hard writing enough to see whether my internal links will work! OK here is the rest of my life story. I went to college in Massachusetts because I wanted an adventure, and I hadn't really been back to the East Coast since I was a little kid. College was pretty neat. My junior year, I studied abroad in Edinburgh, Scotland. I was only supposed to stay for a semester, but I liked it so much I found a way to stay all year.

After college, I wasn't really sure what I wanted to do, so I found a job teaching English as a foreign language in Prague, in the Czech Republic. Prague is a pretty incredible city, and I made a lot of friends from all over the world. I also drank a fair amount of very cheap, very tasty beer.

While I was in Prague, I decided to take the LSATS and apply to Law School. I got in to the University of Washington, so I came back to the States and moved to Seattle. I had only ever been here once before, on a road trip to the Capitol Hill Block Party when I was about 19 years old. Nevertheless, I was convinced it was some kind of mecca, where the sun always shone and everyone wore flannel and big bushy beards. It rains a lot more than I expected, but I was pretty spot on with the rest, I think.

I liked law school, but didn't like being a lawyer. A friend turned me on to coding, and here I am.
